About

After graduating from Georgetown Univesity with a major in Chinese language, I served as a Princeton in Asia fellow based in China.

I served as both an English teacher at a university in Wuhan and a project manager for PlaNet Finance China, the China branch of an international microfinance consultancy based in Beijing. It was during my time designing financial education tools at Planet Finance that I was first exposed to the design processes, and its ability to solve problems beyond the aesthetic.

After returning from China, I continued my study of design, which led me first to a UX design position at Morningstar and to eventually heading up the design department at Avant.

I'm interested in bringing the human-centered design process to industries not traditionally affiliated with design. I also believe strongly in the power of conceptual design to create experiences and objects that question convention.

For anything design related, please feel free to contact me via LinkedIn.
